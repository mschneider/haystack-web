When you use our services, you’re trusting us with your information. We understand this is a big responsibility and work hard to protect your information and put you in control.

This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can update, manage, export, and delete your information.

When you create a Haystack Account, you provide us with personal information that includes your email address and a password. In addition you might provide us with access to third party services that we access on your behalf to generate relevant search results. You can also choose to add payment information to your account. Even if you aren’t signed in to a Haystack Account, you might choose to provide us with information — like an email address to receive updates about our services.

We use your email address, to interact with you directly. For example, we may send you an email for recovering access to your account. Or we may let you know about upcoming changes or improvements to our services. And if you contact Haystack, we’ll keep a record of your request in order to help solve any issues you might be facing.

When creating search results we merely aggregate those from different third-party services you give us access to. We do not access, collect or store your data available through those third-party services for longer than needed to generate the results.

We automatically collect information about the apps, browsers, and devices you use to access Haystack services, which helps us understand how to improve our services.

The information we collect includes unique identifiers, browser type, device type as well as operating system and application version number. We also collect information about the interaction of apps, browsers, and devices with our services, including IP address, crash reports and the date, time, and referrer URL of your request.

We collect information about your activity in our services, which we use to improve the quality of the search results. The activity information we collect may include:

  • Terms you search for
  • Views and interactions with content

You can visit your Haystack Account to find and manage activity information that’s saved in your account.
